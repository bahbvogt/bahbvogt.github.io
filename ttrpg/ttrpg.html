Tabletop System

Hello! This idiosyncratic little TTRPG is inspired variously by Pathfinder, Naruto, Elden Ring, Kill Six Billion Demons, Goblin Punch, world history & mythology, et cetera.

I've posted it here for player reference and for anyone else who may stumble across it. Feel free to run this system at your table, and if you do, let me know how it goes!
