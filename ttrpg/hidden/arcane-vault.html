Hidden Technique

This technique is said to predate the Celestial Triarchs. Precious few sources that mention this technique remain.

Arcane Vault

HP +2 SD 6d6
A Whenever you take a full rest, you may expend 1 spell die to save another unspent spell die in your Vault Seal. You may store a number of spell dice equal to your twice your level in your Vault Seal. As an action, you may choose to release the seal, granting you access to the dice in your Vault Seal for a number of rounds equal to half your level, at which point the seal becomes empty, regardless of whether all dice were spent. While you have access to your Vault Seal, you may spend any amount of spell dice to cast spells, regardless of your level. Your Vault Seal manifests as a small tattoo-like symbol somewhere on your body.
